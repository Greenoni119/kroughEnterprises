Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services.

Krough Enterprises primarily serves the greater San Francisco Bay Area, including San Francisco, Marin County, East Bay, and Silicon Valley. However, we also take on select projects throughout Northern California. Please contact us to discuss your specific location.

The timeline for a custom home project varies depending on the size, complexity, and specific requirements. Generally, the design phase takes 3-6 months, permitting can take 2-4 months, and construction typically takes 10-14 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project during our initial consultation.

Yes, we offer comprehensive architectural and interior design services as part of our full-service approach. Our in-house design team works closely with you to create spaces that reflect your lifestyle, preferences, and needs. We can also collaborate with your existing architect or designer if you prefer.

We provide detailed, transparent project estimates based on your specific requirements. After our initial consultation, we'll develop a preliminary budget range. We believe in clear communication throughout this process to ensure there are no surprises.
