Featured Project: Oakridge Villa

Explore one of our most impressive custom home projects, showcasing our commitment to exceptional design and craftsmanship.

Project Overview

Oakridge Villa is a 4,500 square foot Mediterranean-inspired custom home located in the prestigious Arcadia neighborhood. This exquisite residence features 5 bedrooms, 4.5 bathrooms, a gourmet kitchen, formal dining room, and a spacious great room that opens to a covered outdoor living area.

The client sought a home that would combine traditional Mediterranean design elements with modern functionality and energy efficiency. Our team worked closely with the homeowners from the initial concept through completion to create a residence that perfectly balances aesthetic beauty with practical livability.

Design Elements

The architectural design incorporates classic Mediterranean elements such as a stucco exterior, arched doorways, and a clay tile roof. Inside, the home features custom millwork, marble and hardwood flooring, and a grand staircase with hand-forged iron railings.

The kitchen was designed for serious cooking and entertaining, with professional-grade appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large center island. The primary suite includes a spa-like bathroom with a freestanding tub, oversized shower, and dual vanities.

Construction Excellence

Built with the highest standards of craftsmanship, this home features numerous premium construction details including solid core doors, advanced insulation systems, and high-performance windows. The HVAC system includes zoned climate control for optimal comfort and efficiency.

Outdoors, the property boasts professional landscaping, a covered patio with an outdoor kitchen, and a custom pool and spa area. Every aspect of the project was executed with attention to detail and a commitment to quality that is the hallmark of Krough Enterprises.
